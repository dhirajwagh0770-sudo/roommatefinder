The Roommate finder Website is a web-based platform developed to help users 
find suitable roommates and available rooms easily and quickly. In many cities, 
students and working professionals face difficulties in finding shared 
accommodation, trusted roommates, and verified room details. Traditional 
methods such as brokers, social media posts, and manual searching are time
consuming and often unreliable. 
This system provides a centralized online platform where users can search for 
rooms, post room availability, and connect with potential roommates. Users can 
view room details such as location, rent, facilities, and contact information. The 
system also allows users to post their own room requirements and update 
availability. 
The admin panel helps in managing room posts, verifying data, and maintaining 
system accuracy. This improves transparency and reduces fake listings. Overall, 
the system simplifies the process of finding roommates and shared 
accommodation, saving time and improving convenience for users.
